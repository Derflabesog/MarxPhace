Company Overview

MARX PHACE INVESTMENTS SALUTIONS is a Namibian-owned civil engineering and construction company providing professional engineering-led construction solutions. The company delivers building construction, civil works, road works, and project management services across Namibia.

Vision

To be a trusted civil engineering and construction partner delivering sustainable infrastructure and quality developments across Namibia.

Mission

To deliver safe, cost-effective, and high-quality civil engineering and construction projects through professional expertise, integrity, and innovation.

Core Values

  • Professional Integrity
  • Engineering Excellence
  • Safety & Quality
  • Client Satisfaction
  • Timely Delivery

Director & Lead Civil Engineer

The company is led by a qualified Civil Engineer with experience in building construction, civil engineering works, road construction, and project supervision. All projects are executed under direct engineering oversight to ensure compliance with national standards and best practices.
